We find ways to make the Internet better

Web Security

Improving the security of the web, by examining the weaknesses of TLS and the Certificate Authority system. We are interested in alternative security mechanisms and enhanced usability of our current system.

Sentiment Analysis

Helping to understand political and social change in the Middle East by automatically learning the sentiment of political tweets in Farsi.

Internet Measurements

Measuring the Internet to provide insight into how people use the network for a variety of purposes, both good and bad.

Personal Cloud Computing

Designing a cloud for your home, connecting all your devices and providing seamless access to all your data. Emphasis on performance, security, and usability.
