Cloud Storage

We are interested in studying user perceptions and behavior with respect to cloud storage services. In particular, we are interested in behaviors such as which services are used, what types of data are stored, and how collaboration and sharing are performed. We are also investigating user attitudes toward cloud storage on topics such as payment, pri- vacy, security, and robustness.

We are currently conducting interviews with people in the Provo area.
